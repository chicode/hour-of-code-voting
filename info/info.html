Helpful sites

  • MDN (mozilla developer network): very specific and detailed information on all the things in web development. This is by far the most reliable source. Click on this when you want information about a specific thing.
  • w3schools: like MDN, but a little more beginner friendly.
  • stack overflow: specific answers to specific questions, probably the most popliar site on this list. Click on this when you want to do something specific, or have a specific issue/error.
  • Quora: like stack overflow but more general and a little more friendly :).
